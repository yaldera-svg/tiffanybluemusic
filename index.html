Music Style

Icebreaker is an electronic pop project focused on harsh noise and metallic production.

The music is meant to feel robotic and atmospheric, inspired by cold colors and futuristic themes.

Key Elements

Minimal Sound

The songs avoid heavy layering and instead focus on a few clear sounds.

Independent Releases

Music is released as singles rather than large albums.

Visual Identity

The theme uses icy blue colors to match the sound of the music.

Featured Track

The track “Melted” represents the overall sound of the project.
